HOW THINGS STARTED


When my mom was throwing herself a birthday party, she asked me to take care of the drinks that night. Since I take every job very seriously, I decided to build a little cocktail stand. Since I had so much fun, and people got extremely enthousiastic over my cocktails, I decided to commercialize the concept. And so, this is what happened!

THIS IS WHAT WE DO


Wondering what “Mix” can mean to you? Here’s the deal: this brand new cocktail caravan can take your event to the next level! Our mobile bar does serve the most delicious cocktails and we do it in style. It’s not only about the drinks, it’s about the entire experience.
